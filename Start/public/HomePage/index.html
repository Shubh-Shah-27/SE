Our Vision

PlacementPrep is aimed to become every student’s first choice for their placement preparation. With our simple and engaging User Interface, we want to make those anxious hours convenient and satisfying for you. With questions from all important domains and detailed analysis reports, we plan to help you understand your weak points. Our suggested articles and tutorials will assist you in working on those topics and thereby strengthen your knowledge and performance. Our ultimate vision is to make this a platform for gaining knowledge as well as sharing experiences; a community where students help each other to excel together.

Features

We will provide a question bank for every important domain tested in an interview and also provide a Quiz feature to practice and revise the same. Using the data from the quizzes attempted by the user, we will analyze and provide a detailed report showing the user’s performance in various domains. Using the analysis report, we will recommend resources to learn and practice. Users can also use this section to learn and practice whichever topic he wants. Users can share their interview experiences and thereby grow together by helping each other. They can also connect to people working in the industry to gain insights about their future jobs.
